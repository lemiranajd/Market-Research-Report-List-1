<p><ul><li>FE-SEM</li><li>FIB-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) is crucial in the semiconductor market, with key types including Field Emission SEM (FE-SEM) and Focused Ion Beam SEM (FIB-SEM). FE-SEM offers high-resolution imaging due to its cold cathode electron source, making it suitable for nanoscale analysis. On the other hand, FIB-SEM combines imaging and material modification, enabling precise milling and layer analysis. Both techniques play vital roles in semiconductor manufacturing and research, facilitating advanced inspections, defect analysis, and material characterization.</p></p>
