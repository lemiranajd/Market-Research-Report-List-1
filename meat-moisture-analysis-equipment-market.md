<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meat moisture analysis equipment market includes key technologies like DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology utilizes X-ray beams to accurately measure fat and moisture levels, providing detailed compositional analysis. In contrast, NIR technology employs light reflection to assess moisture content rapidly and non-destructively. Both methods enhance quality control in meat processing, ensuring product consistency and compliance with industry standards while improving overall production efficiency and reducing waste.</p></p>
