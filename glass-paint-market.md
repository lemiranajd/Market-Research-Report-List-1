<p><strong>The Glass Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass paint market consists of two primary types: water-based and solvent-based paints. Water-based glass paints utilize water as the primary solvent, offering low odor, easy cleanup, and environmental benefits. They tend to dry quickly and are suitable for indoor applications. In contrast, solvent-based glass paints contain organic solvents, providing enhanced durability, gloss, and resistance to harsh conditions. However, they can emit stronger odors and require more careful handling. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and application needs.</p></p>
