<p><strong>The Indoor Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active DAS</li><li>Passive DAS</li><li>Hybrid DAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indoor Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) enhance wireless coverage in buildings by distributing signals from antennas throughout the space. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Active DAS** uses powered antennas connected to a central controller, providing high flexibility and capacity. </p><p>2. **Passive DAS** employs coaxial cables and antennas without active components, offering simplicity and lower costs. </p><p>3. **Hybrid DAS** combines both active and passive elements, optimizing performance for varying needs and environments, ensuring robust connectivity across diverse spaces.</p></p>
