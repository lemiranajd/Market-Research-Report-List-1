<p><p>The Pneumatic Tourniquet System market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Bladder Tourniquet and Dual Bladder Tourniquet. The Single Bladder Tourniquet features one inflatable bladder that applies pressure to restrict blood flow to a limb, making it suitable for various surgical procedures. In contrast, the Dual Bladder Tourniquet consists of two bladders, enabling more precise control of pressure and better distribution, which is advantageous in complex surgeries requiring enhanced safety and effectiveness. Both systems are essential for minimizing blood loss during operations.</p></p>
