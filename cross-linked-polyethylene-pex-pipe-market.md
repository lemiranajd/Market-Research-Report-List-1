<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cross-linked Polyethylene (PEX) Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEX-A</li><li>PEX-B</li><li>PEX-C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cross-linked Polyethylene (PEX) pipe market is categorized into three types: PEX-A, PEX-B, and PEX-C. PEX-A is produced using the Engel method, offering superior flexibility and resistance to cracking. PEX-B, made through the dry method, is more economical and has enhanced chlorine resistance, but is slightly less flexible. PEX-C, created by irradiation cross-linking, provides good strength and thermal resistance, though it is the least flexible. Each type serves specific applications in plumbing and heating systems based on these characteristics.</p></p>
