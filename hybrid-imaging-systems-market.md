<p><p>Hybrid imaging systems integrate two imaging modalities to enhance diagnostic accuracy and improve patient outcomes. The PET/CT market combines positron emission tomography with computed tomography, allowing for precise localization of metabolic activity within anatomical structures. SPECT/CT merges single-photon emission computed tomography with CT imaging, providing functional and structural information for various conditions. PET/MRI pairs positron emission tomography with magnetic resonance imaging, offering superior soft tissue contrast and functional imaging, making it valuable in oncology and neurology applications.</p></p>
