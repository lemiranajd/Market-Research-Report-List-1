<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Detectors</li><li>Ionization Smoke Detectors</li><li>Combination Smoke Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke detectors market is categorized into three main types: photoelectric smoke detectors, which use a light source to detect smoke particles; ionization smoke detectors, which utilize radioactive material to sense smoke through ionization disruption; and combination smoke detectors, which integrate both technologies for enhanced detection. Each type serves different environments and smoke types, addressing specific safety needs. The choice between them can be influenced by factors like response time, sensitivity to various smoke conditions, and installation preferences.</p></p>
