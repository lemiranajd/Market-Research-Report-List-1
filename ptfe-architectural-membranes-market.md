<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE Architectural Membranes market is segmented into hydrophobic and hydrophilic membranes, each serving distinct applications. Hydrophobic PTFE membranes repel water, making them ideal for weather-resistant structures and applications requiring moisture protection. Conversely, hydrophilic PTFE membranes are designed to allow water vapor to pass through, making them suitable for applications requiring breathability, such as in building facades where moisture management is crucial. Both types enhance architectural aesthetics and functionality, offering durability and flexibility in design.</p></p>
