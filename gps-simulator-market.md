<p><p>The GPS simulator market is divided into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel simulators focus on generating signals for one GPS receiver at a time, making them suitable for basic testing applications. In contrast, multi-channel simulators can simulate multiple satellites simultaneously, allowing for more complex scenarios and comprehensive testing for devices that require advanced navigation capabilities. This distinction helps users choose the right simulator based on their specific testing needs and project requirements.</p></p>
