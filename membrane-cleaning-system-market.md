<p><strong>The Membrane Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flushing</li><li>Backwash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Membrane Cleaning System market is primarily segmented into two types: flushing and backwash. Flushing involves the flow of cleaning solutions or water through the membrane to remove fouling and contaminants, ensuring optimal performance. Backwash, on the other hand, reverses the flow of water through the membrane to dislodge particles caught in the membrane pores. Both methods are essential for maintaining membrane efficiency and longevity, catering to various applications in water treatment and industrial processes.</p></p>
