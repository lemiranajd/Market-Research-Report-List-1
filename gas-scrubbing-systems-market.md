<p><ul><li>Jet & Venturi Scrubbers</li><li>Orifice Scrubbers</li><li>Packed Bed Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas scrubbing systems are vital for controlling air pollutants by removing contaminants from gas streams. Jet and venturi scrubbers utilize high-velocity gas flow to create a suction effect, capturing particles and gases. Orifice scrubbers operate through structured orifices that promote gas-liquid interaction for effective scrubbing. Packed bed scrubbers use a bed of packing material to maximize gas-liquid contact, enhancing mass transfer and pollutant removal. Each type offers distinct advantages for various industrial applications, ensuring regulatory compliance and environmental protection.</p></p>
