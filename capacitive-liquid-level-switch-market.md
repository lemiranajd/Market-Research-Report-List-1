<p><p>The capacitive liquid level switch market features two primary types: flange and screw types. Flange-type switches are designed for easy installation on tanks or containers, providing a robust connection and facilitating maintenance. They are often used in large industrial applications. Screw-type switches, on the other hand, are compact and can be directly threaded into tanks, making them suitable for smaller spaces and portable setups. Both types utilize capacitance to detect liquid levels accurately, offering reliable performance in various industries.</p></p>
