<p><strong>The Hot Melt Grade Petroleum Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C5/C9 Petroleum Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot melt grade petroleum resin market comprises three primary types: C5, C9, and C5/C9 petroleum resins. C5 petroleum resin, derived from C5 hydrocarbon fractions, offers excellent adhesive properties and is commonly used in pressure-sensitive adhesives. C9 petroleum resin, sourced from C9 fractions, provides high thermal stability and is ideal for coatings and sealants. C5/C9 petroleum resin, a blend of both types, combines the advantages of each, making it versatile for various applications, including inks and road marking materials.</p></p>
