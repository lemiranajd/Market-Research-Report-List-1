<p><p>Core balance current transformers (CBCTs) are essential for detecting earth fault currents in electrical systems. The Split Type CBCT comprises two halves that can encircle existing cables, facilitating easy installation without disruption. In contrast, the Through Type CBCT allows conductors to pass through a central core, often requiring more space but providing reliable measurements. Both types serve similar functions but differ in installation methods and suitability for various applications, catering to the needs of diverse electrical environments.</p></p>
