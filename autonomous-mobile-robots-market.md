<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) market can be categorized into two primary types: LiDAR-based and LiDAR+Vision-based. LiDAR-based AMRs utilize laser technology to create detailed 2D or 3D maps of their environment, allowing for precise navigation and obstacle avoidance. In contrast, LiDAR+Vision-based AMRs combine LiDAR with camera systems, enhancing perception capabilities through visual data analysis. This integration provides improved recognition of complex environments, facilitating better decision-making and increased adaptability in dynamic and cluttered spaces.</p></p>
