<p><ul><li>Vertebroplasty</li><li>Kyphoplasty</li><li>Radiofrequency Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interventional spine devices market comprises various treatment types, including vertebroplasty, kyphoplasty, and radiofrequency ablation. Vertebroplasty involves the injection of bone cement into fractured vertebrae, providing stability and pain relief. Kyphoplasty is similar but includes the inflation of a balloon to create space before cement injection, restoring vertebral height. Radiofrequency ablation targets nerve tissues with heat to alleviate chronic back pain. Together, these techniques enhance spinal health and improve patient quality of life through minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
