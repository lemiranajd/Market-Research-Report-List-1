<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Piston Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric pressure sensor market is primarily categorized into diaphragm type and piston type sensors. Diaphragm-type sensors use a flexible membrane that flexes under pressure, generating a voltage proportional to the applied force. They are widely used for measuring low to medium pressure ranges. Piston-type sensors utilize a solid piston mechanism that applies force to a piezoelectric crystal, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types offer high accuracy and rapid response times, catering to various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
