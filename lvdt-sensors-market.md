<p><strong>The LVDT Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC LVDTs</li><li>DC LVDTs</li><li>Spring Loaded LVDTs / Gageheads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) sensors are vital in measuring linear displacement and are categorized into AC LVDTs, DC LVDTs, and spring-loaded LVDTs/gageheads. AC LVDTs use alternating current for high accuracy applications, while DC LVDTs operate on direct current, providing advantages in specific conditions. Spring-loaded LVDTs, also known as gageheads, feature a built-in spring mechanism, ensuring consistent contact with the measured object. These distinct types cater to varied industries, including aerospace, manufacturing, and automation, enhancing precision in measurement tasks.</p></p>
