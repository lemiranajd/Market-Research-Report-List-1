<p><p>The semi-permeable film market encompasses two primary types: biomembranes and synthetic membranes. Biomembranes are naturally occurring membranes, often derived from biological materials, known for their selective permeability and biocompatibility, making them suitable for medical and biotechnological applications. Synthetic membranes, on the other hand, are artificially created using polymers and other materials, offering tailored properties for industrial processes like desalination, filtration, and separation technologies. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, driven by growing demands for efficient separation solutions.</p></p>
