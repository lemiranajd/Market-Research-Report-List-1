<p><p>The ceramic scintillators market comprises two primary types: ordinary ceramic scintillators and transparent ceramic scintillators. Ordinary ceramic scintillators are widely used for radiation detection and imaging due to their solid-state structure and durability. They typically exhibit excellent light output and energy resolution. In contrast, transparent ceramic scintillators are designed for specialized applications where optical clarity is essential, allowing for efficient light transmission and enhanced performance in advanced imaging systems. Both types are crucial for various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
