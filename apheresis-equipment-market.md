<p><p>Apheresis equipment is used for separating blood components, playing a crucial role in therapeutic procedures. The market features two primary types: centrifugation and membrane filtration. Centrifugation employs speed and centrifugal force to separate blood components based on density, efficiently isolating platelets, plasma, or red blood cells. In contrast, membrane filtration utilizes selective membranes to separate components based on size, enabling more precise extraction of desired elements. Both methods are essential for medical practices like blood donations and treatments.</p></p>
