Corticosteroids reduce inflammation and immune response. Proton pump inhibitors manage gastrointestinal symptoms linked to mast cell activity by lowering stomach acid. Immunomodulators adjust the immune system's response for better control of mast cell proliferation. Mast cell stabilizers prevent degranulation, helping to manage symptoms effectively. Together, these treatments support patients in managing cutaneous mastocytosis.</p></p>
