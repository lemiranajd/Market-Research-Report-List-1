<p><strong>The Cable Managers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Cable Managers</li><li>Horizontal Cable Managers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable managers market is primarily divided into vertical and horizontal cable managers, each serving distinct purposes in organized cable management. Vertical cable managers are designed to route cables between different racks, enhancing airflow and access while maximizing space efficiency in data centers. Horizontal cable managers, on the other hand, organize cables along the front or back of equipment racks, ensuring neatness and preventing tangles. Both types are essential for maintaining an orderly environment and optimizing equipment performance in IT infrastructure.</p></p>
