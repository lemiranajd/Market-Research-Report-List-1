<p><p>The test rig market includes various types, with hydraulic test rigs and hydrostatic test rigs being prominent. Hydraulic test rigs are designed to assess the performance and integrity of mechanical components using high-pressure hydraulic fluid, ensuring their ability to withstand operational conditions. In contrast, hydrostatic test rigs focus specifically on testing the leak integrity and strength of vessels or pipelines by applying fluid pressure to check for weaknesses. Both are crucial for safety and reliability in industries like aerospace, automotive, and structural engineering.</p></p>
