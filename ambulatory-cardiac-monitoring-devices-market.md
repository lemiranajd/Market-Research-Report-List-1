<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring devices include various types tailored for continuous heart rhythm assessment. Holter monitors record continuous ECG data over 24-48 hours, capturing irregularities during daily activities. Event monitors are designed for intermittent use, allowing patients to record symptoms as they occur over weeks. Mobile cardiac telemetry provides real-time ECG monitoring through wireless technology, enabling immediate data transmission to healthcare providers. Other devices may include sleep monitors or implantable loop recorders, catering to diverse patient needs in cardiac care.</p></p>
