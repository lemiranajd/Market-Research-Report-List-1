<p><p>The waste paper baler market is primarily categorized into two types: horizontal and vertical balers. Horizontal balers are designed for high-capacity operations, processing large volumes of waste paper efficiently, making them ideal for industrial settings. They maximize space and allow for automated feeding. In contrast, vertical balers are compact and better suited for smaller operations or facilities with limited space. These balers require manual loading, and while they handle smaller volumes, they are cost-effective and widely used in retail and smaller recycling centers.</p></p>
