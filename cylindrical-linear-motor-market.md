<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Asynchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cylindrical linear motor market is divided into two main types: synchronous and asynchronous motors. Synchronous linear motors operate at a constant speed, maintaining a fixed relationship between the magnetic field and the rotor position, making them ideal for precision applications. In contrast, asynchronous linear motors function without this fixed relationship, allowing for greater variability in speed and torque, which makes them suitable for a wider range of industrial applications. Both types cater to different technological needs in automation and motion control.</p></p>
