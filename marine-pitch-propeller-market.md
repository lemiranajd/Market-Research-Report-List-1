<p><ul><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine pitch propeller market primarily consists of two types: fixed pitch propellers and controllable pitch propellers. Fixed pitch propellers have blades set at a specific angle, optimizing performance for particular speeds but lacking adaptability. In contrast, controllable pitch propellers allow for blade angle adjustments while in operation, providing greater maneuverability, efficiency, and responsiveness to varying marine conditions. Both types serve distinct applications across commercial, naval, and recreational vessels, catering to different performance and operational requirements.</p></p>
