<p><p>The spine devices market is primarily categorized into spinal fusion products and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion products are used to stabilize and join vertebrae, often through implants like rods, screws, and bone grafts, to treat conditions such as degenerative disc disease. Non-fusion products, on the other hand, include devices like interspinous spacers and dynamic stabilization systems, which aim to provide support without permanently fusing bones. Both types address diverse spinal disorders, enhancing patient mobility and reducing pain.</p></p>
