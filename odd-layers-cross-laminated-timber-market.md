<p><p>The Odd Layers Cross Laminated Timber (CLT) market encompasses two primary types: adhesive-bonded and mechanically fastened systems. Adhesive-bonded CLT relies on strong adhesives to bond layers of timber, providing structural integrity and durability. This method offers superior moisture resistance and load-bearing capacity. In contrast, mechanically fastened CLT utilizes mechanical connectors like screws or nails, enabling easier assembly and disassembly. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, emphasizing sustainability and efficient use of timber resources in building applications.</p></p>
