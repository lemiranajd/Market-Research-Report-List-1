<p><ul><li>Glaucoma Drainage Devices</li><li>Glaucoma Laser Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma surgical devices market encompasses various technologies aimed at managing intraocular pressure in glaucoma patients. Glaucoma drainage devices, such as trabeculectomy tubes and shunts, facilitate fluid outflow to lower pressure. On the other hand, glaucoma laser systems, including selective laser trabeculoplasty and laser peripheral iridotomy, utilize targeted laser energy to enhance drainage and reduce pressure without invasive surgery. Both types play crucial roles in improving patient outcomes and preserving vision in individuals affected by glaucoma.</p></p>
